Energy-Saving Tips

  • Set your thermostat to 68°F (20°C) when you're home and awake, and lower when you're asleep or away to save on heating costs. (pse.com)
  • Seal air leaks around windows and doors using caulk or weatherstripping to prevent heat loss. (energy.gov)
  • Replace incandescent bulbs with ENERGY STAR® certified LED bulbs, which use up to 90% less energy and last 15 times longer. (energystar.gov)
  • Use ceiling fans to circulate warm air during winter, allowing you to lower your thermostat setting without reducing comfort. (directenergy.com)
  • Unplug electronic devices when not in use to eliminate "vampire" energy consumption from appliances that draw power even when turned off. (thesun.co.uk)
